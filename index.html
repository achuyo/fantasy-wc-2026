Official rules
01
Self-registration
Anyone can join by going to the Players tab, entering their name, and dragging the punishment mixer. Then go to Picks, select your name, and tap your nation. Each nation is first-come, first-served and locked once chosen. You cannot change your pick without contacting the commissioner.
02
No manual score editing
All points are calculated automatically from the official API. Nobody can enter, edit, or override scores — prevents mistakes and cheating entirely.
03
Auto-sync every 30 minutes
The app fetches real World Cup results every 30 minutes while open. Points update silently for everyone. Commissioner can also Force Sync at any time.
04
Hybrid punishment mixer
Each player drags their own split at registration. Exchange rate: $1.25 = 1 shot = 15 push-ups (full = $25 / 20 shots / 300 push-ups). Any combo valid. Values rounded to whole numbers. Locked once tournament begins.
05
Who pays up
Bottom player(s) in final standings pay their full custom mix. If tied for last, all tied players pay. Commissioner has final say on edge cases.
06
Tiebreakers
(1) Total goals scored by your nation, (2) Tournament stage reached, (3) Coin flip by commissioner.
07
Shots rule
All shots in one session within 1 week of the final. Non-drinker alternative: equal hot sauce shots. No skipping.
08
Push-ups rule
All push-ups in one session, witnessed by 2+ players. Rest between sets allowed. Breaks over 5 min reset the current set. No partial credit.
09
Commissioner controls
Only the commissioner (PIN protected) can add/remove players, assign picks, issue punishments, and force sync scores. Ruling on disputes is final.
10
Have fun
Friendly league only. Pay debts with good humour and enjoy the beautiful game. ⚽
