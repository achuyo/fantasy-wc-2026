Official rules
01
One team per player
Each participant picks exactly one World Cup nation. No duplicates. Standings update the moment a pick is made.
02
No manual score editing
All points are calculated automatically from the official API. Nobody can enter, edit, or override scores. This prevents mistakes and cheating entirely.
03
Auto-sync every 30 minutes
The app fetches real World Cup results every 30 minutes while open. Points update silently for everyone. You can also Force Sync any time in the Live Scores tab.
04
Hybrid punishment mixer
Each player drags their own split at registration. Exchange rate: $1 = 1 shot = 15 push-ups. Any combo valid including all-in on one type. Locked once tournament begins.
05
Who pays up
Bottom player(s) in final standings pay their full custom punishment. If tied for last, all tied players pay. Commissioner has final say on any edge cases.
06
Tiebreakers
(1) Total goals scored by your nation, (2) Tournament stage reached, (3) Coin flip by commissioner.
07
Shots rule
All shots in one session within 1 week of the final. Non-drinker alternative: equal hot sauce shots. No skipping.
08
Push-ups rule
All push-ups in one session, witnessed by 2+ players. Rest between sets is fine. Breaks over 5 min reset the current set. No partial credit.
09
Have fun
Friendly league only. Pay debts with good humour and enjoy the beautiful game. ⚽
