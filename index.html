Official rules
01
One team per player
Each participant picks exactly one World Cup nation. No duplicates. Picks lock once confirmed. Standings update automatically when a pick is made.
02
Pick deadline
All picks must be in before June 11, 2026. Late picks get a random remaining nation assigned by the commissioner.
03
Hybrid punishment mixer
Each player drags their own split at registration. Any combination is valid — including going all-in on one type or setting others to zero. Exchange rate: $1 = 1 shot = 15 push-ups. Total is always 20 units. Locked once tournament begins.
04
Who pays up
Bottom player(s) in final standings pay their full custom mix. If tied for last, all tied players pay.
05
Tiebreakers
(1) Total goals by your nation, (2) Tournament stage reached, (3) Coin flip by commissioner.
06
Shots rule
All shots in one session within 1 week of the final. Non-drinker alternative: equal hot sauce shots. No skipping.
07
Push-ups rule
All push-ups in one session, witnessed by 2+ players. Rest between sets allowed. Breaks over 5 min reset the current set. No partial credit.
08
Commissioner is final arbiter
All disputes go to the commissioner. Ruling is final. Bribery encouraged but not guaranteed to work.
09
Have fun
Friendly league only. Pay debts with good humour and enjoy the beautiful game. ⚽
