4. Commissioner controls — Use this hub to track everything and issue punishments.

Official rules
Rule 01
One team per player
Each participant picks exactly one World Cup nation. No two players can pick the same country. Picks are first-come, first-served and locked once confirmed by the commissioner.
Rule 02
Pick deadline
All picks must be submitted before June 11, 2026 (kickoff of the first match). Late picks will be assigned a random remaining nation by the commissioner.
Rule 03
Punishment scale is equal
$20 cash, 20 shots, and 300 push-ups are treated as equivalent punishment tiers. Each player chooses their punishment type when registering. This cannot be changed after the tournament begins.
Rule 04
Who pays up
The bottom player(s) in the final standings pay a full punishment. If multiple players tie for last, they all pay. The commissioner has final say on tiebreakers.
Rule 05
Tiebreaker order
Ties broken in this order: (1) total goals scored by your nation, (2) tournament stage reached, (3) coin flip by the commissioner if still tied.
Rule 06
Shots rule
Shots must be consumed in a single session within 1 week of the final. Non-drinker alternative: 20 non-alcoholic shots (pickle juice, hot sauce, etc.) — commissioner decides. No skipping.
Rule 07
Push-ups rule
300 push-ups must be completed in one session, witnessed by at least two other players. Rest between sets is allowed. Breaks longer than 5 minutes reset the current set. No partial credit.
Rule 08
Commissioner is final arbiter
All disputes, edge cases, and complaints go to the commissioner. The commissioner's ruling is final and non-negotiable. Bribery is encouraged but not guaranteed to work.
Rule 09
Have fun
This is a friendly league. No actual gambling is sanctioned. Keep it light, pay your debts with good humour, and enjoy the beautiful game.
