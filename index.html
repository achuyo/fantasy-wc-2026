Official rules
01
One team per player
Each participant picks exactly one World Cup nation. No duplicates. Picks are locked once confirmed by the commissioner.
02
Pick deadline
All picks must be in before June 11, 2026. Late picks get a random remaining nation assigned by the commissioner.
03
Punishment scale is equal
$20 cash, 20 shots, and 300 push-ups are equivalent tiers. Players choose when registering — locked once tournament begins.
04
Who pays up
Bottom player(s) in final standings pay a full punishment. If tied for last, all tied players pay. Commissioner has final say.
05
Tiebreakers
(1) Total goals scored by your nation, (2) Tournament stage reached, (3) Coin flip by commissioner.
06
Shots rule
20 shots in one session within 1 week of the final. Non-drinker alternative: 20 non-alcoholic shots — commissioner decides. No skipping.
07
Push-ups rule
300 push-ups in one session, witnessed by 2+ players. Rest between sets is fine. Breaks over 5 minutes reset the current set.
08
Commissioner is final arbiter
All disputes go to the commissioner. Ruling is final. Bribery is encouraged but not guaranteed to work.
09
Have fun
Friendly league only. Pay your debts with good humour and enjoy the beautiful game. ⚽
